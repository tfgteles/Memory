Memory Puzzle

Memory Puzzle Instructions

  • Turn over two cards by clicking
  • If the two cards match, you hit them, otherwise you miss the strike
  • The uncover pair will be turned over when you click to uncover the next pair
  • Remember what was on each card and where it was
  • You win, if you hit all pairs, but the number of misses must be less than the number of total pairs, otherwise, you lost the game
